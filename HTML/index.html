A different
kind of music

Ashref is a singer-songwriter from Tunisia whose versatile voice and songwriting have found new heights in Budapest’s vibrant music scene. After refining his sound, he began collaborating with UK producer anatu on projects alongside Zubi, Arif Erdem Ocak, Dubem K, and on the track "Breathing" with Kállay Saunders.
Having embarked on a journey to shape his own musical identity, Ashref has developed a sound that resonates with listeners across the globe. His collaboration with Zubi and anatu on "Weight" stands as a milestone in his career, amassing over 5.5 million streams on Spotify and exceeding 8 million views on YouTube. The song highlights not only his emotive vocal style but also his ability to craft and contribute to chart-topping hits.
Singing in English and occasionally weaving in French and Arabic to bring an extra layer of richness and soul to his music, Ashref moves effortlessly from the soft, soaring tones of R&B ballads to a raw, passionate delivery when the song demands it. With numerous solo projects and collaborations underway, Ashref continues to build his path independently — always remembering the early stages where he first shared his voice with the world.
